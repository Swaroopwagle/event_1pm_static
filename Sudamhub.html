Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our cloud migration services.

The timeline depends on the complexity and scale of your infrastructure. A simple migration can take 4-6 weeks, while larger, more complex migrations can take 3-6 months. During our initial assessment, we provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

Costs vary based on the scope of work, size of infrastructure, and specific requirements. We offer transparent pricing with a fixed-fee model for predictable budgeting. Typically, our clients see a 30-50% reduction in overall IT costs after migration due to cloud efficiencies.

We implement end-to-end encryption for all data in transit, use secure transfer protocols, and conduct security assessments before, during, and after migration. Our team follows industry best practices and compliance standards (HIPAA, GDPR, PCI-DSS) to ensure your data remains protected throughout the process.

We recommend and typically implement a phased migration approach. This allows us to migrate less critical systems first, learn from the process, and then migrate mission-critical systems with greater confidence. This approach minimizes risk and allows for business continuity throughout the migration process.
